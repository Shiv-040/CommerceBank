Here are some of Kansas City's sports

Kansas City Chiefs

The Kansas City Chiefs is one of Kansas City's most valued sports team. With their recent superbowl win and charasmatic personality it's hard to not be a fan of the chiefs. It's a great team to support and a great way to spend time with family and go out with friends!

Address: 1 Arrowhead Dr, Kansas City, MO 64129

Cost: $198.00

Uniqueness: Known as the loudest stadiums in football

Royals Kansas City

One can't be in Kansas City without thinking about the Kansas City Royals. With baseball being a staple in Kansas City culture it is hard to not be a fan of the Royals and all the rich history that comes along with it. Just like the football games it's a great way to spend time with family and watch some good ole basebalL!

Address: 1 Arrowhead Dr, Kansas City, MO 64129

Cost: $82

Uniqueness: Instead of scouting the team would take non-baseball players and train them. But not before making some of the best baseball players in the league.

Sporting Kansas City

Last but not least we have Sporting Kansas City and the blue devils. If you are a soccer fan then this is a place for you. With great players and a wonderful culture around the sport and team you will find yourself getting invested into the beloved Blue Devils.

Address: 1 Sporting Way, Kansas City, KS 66111

Cost: $37.00

Uniqueness: They were first known as the Kansas City Wizards before they were known as the Blue Devils.
