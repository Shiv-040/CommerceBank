Here are some of Kansas Cities best Adventure Parks

Kansas City Zoo

Opened in 1909, The Kansas City zoo is a great place to take your family for the day! The zoo is home to more then 1300 animals with animals ranging from penguins to camels. There are many things to do besides just look at animals, there are activites that the staff hold everyday having something to do for everyone.

Address: 6800 Zoo Dr, Kansas City, MO 64132

Cost: $20.00

Uniqueness: 1300 Animals all in one place with many activites for everyone!

Worlds of Fun

Worlds of Fun is Kansas City's biggest and most popular Adventure park, its over 235 acres of pure fun, there are over 50 rides and over 100 activites to do. There are many resturants and snacks in the park allowing you to have a fun filled day! For the kids there is Snoopy land where kids can go on rides. During October the place shifts to a 236 acre haunted house.

Address: 4545 Worlds of Fun Ave, Kansas City, MO 64161

Cost: $35.00

Uniqueness: Many rides and will change depending on time of year you go!

Go Ape Zipline

Go Ape Zipline is a park that you can zip line though in a forest, you strap in and you just enjoy the scenery. You may want to consider the season you go in as this is in the middle of a forest. They also do night time trips with glowsticks and lights.

Address: 7331 Oakwood Dr, Kansas City, MO 64132

Cost: $60.00

Uniqueness: They even have night time activites where the enviorment is light up!

Legoland

If you are looking for activites for your kids, this is the place for you! There are things from a lego playground to a virtual reality lego center. In addition this is in the same building as sealife so if you have time you can see both!

Address: 2475 Grand Blvd, Kansas City, MO 64108

Cost: $30.00

Uniqueness: They are right next to Sea life, so if you want to see both it is very convenient!
